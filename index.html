Health Centre has a story of evolution which perhaps makes it a unique medical facility among others. And the uniqueness is that it was born out of an urge to give medical care to its initial patients. it was the brainchild of an association of senior general medical practitioners and specialists. They were serving the patients in calicut with all that they can and yet when time of advanced care came, they had to refer their dear patients to far away facilities in town. Some facilities were as far as Manipal and the doctors were quite aggrieved to see their patients having to take such pains in getting quality treatment..
